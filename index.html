Introduction

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! This website is a showcase and archive of my ongoing journey to explore the vast worlds of programming, data science, mathematics, and visual analytics. As a data enthusiast, I am dedicated to unraveling the stories hidden within complex datasets and presenting compelling visual narratives. Whether you're a fellow data explorer, a curious mind, or simply someone intrigued by the power of information, I hope my collection of projects and articles will both inspire and deepen your appreciation for the world of data-driven discovery.

Portfolio

I am thrilled to present my working portfolio. In these pages, you will witness my growth from a curious learner to a budding data scientist, as I've harnessed the power of data to tackle intriguing conundrums and real-world challenges. So, let's embark on this data-driven adventure and discover the stories that data has to tell.
